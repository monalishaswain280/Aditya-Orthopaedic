Welcome to Aditya Orthopedic & Trauma Center

Your trusted destination for advanced orthopedic care, trauma management, and comprehensive rehabilitation.

Welcome to Aditya Orthopedic and Trauma Center, the leading healthcare facility in Tumkur. Renowned for our exceptional services and patient-centric approach, we are committed to delivering the highest standards of care. Located in the heart of Tumkur, our advanced center is equipped with modern technology and led by a team of expert doctors and skilled professionals dedicated to your health and well-being. We specialize in orthopaedic and trauma care, including joint replacement, fracture management, and spine surgery. Our services also include general surgery, laparoscopy, plastic surgery, neurology, neurosurgery, paediatric and neonatology care with a fully equipped NICU, and physiotherapy and rehabilitation, ensuring comprehensive care for all patients.
