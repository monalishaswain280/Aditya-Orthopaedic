Services

  • Services

Pediatric & Neonatology

Our pediatric and neonatology department provides comprehensive care for children, from infancy to adolescence, with a focus on early detection and treatment. Our fully equipped NICU ensures that newborns, especially premature or critically ill infants, receive the best possible care for optimal health outcomes.

Urology and Andrology

Our urology and andrology services address conditions related to the urinary tract and male reproductive system. From kidney stones to prostate issues and male infertility, we offer personalized care and advanced treatments to improve patients’ health and quality of life.

Trauma & Emergency

Our 24-hour trauma and emergency department is equipped to handle critical and life-threatening situations. Whether it’s an accident, heart attack, or other urgent medical conditions, our team of experts provides immediate and comprehensive care to stabilize and treat patients swiftly.

Neuro Surgery & Spine Surgery

Our neurosurgery and spine surgery department offers specialized care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders. We provide advanced surgical interventions for conditions like tumors, spinal cord injuries, and degenerative diseases, helping patients regain mobility and improve their neurological health.

Plastic Surgery

Our plastic surgery department focuses on both reconstructive and cosmetic procedures, aimed at restoring function and appearance. Whether it's for post-trauma repair, congenital conditions, or aesthetic enhancement, our skilled surgeons use the latest techniques to deliver the best results for our patients.

Surgical Services

Our surgical department provides a range of services, including endoscopic and laparoscopic surgery, ensuring minimally invasive procedures that promote faster recovery and less post-operative discomfort. Our highly experienced surgeons perform a wide variety of surgeries with precision and care.

Rheumatology

Our rheumatology department specializes in diagnosing and treating autoimmune diseases and inflammatory conditions, such as arthritis and lupus. Using advanced treatment methods, we help manage pain, reduce inflammation, and improve mobility, enhancing the quality of life for our patients.

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Our physiotherapy and rehabilitation center offers personalized treatment plans to help patients recover from surgery, injury, or illness. We use a range of techniques such as manual therapy, exercises, and modalities to restore function, alleviate pain, and improve strength and flexibility.

Critical Care and Emergency Services

Our Critical Care and Emergency Services provide immediate care for acute conditions like trauma, cardiac emergencies, and respiratory failure. With a dedicated team and advanced technology, we ensure rapid intervention and stabilization to deliver the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Diagnostic and Imaging Services

Our Diagnostic and Imaging Services provide accurate and timely results using state-of-the-art equipment such as X-ray, MRI, ultrasound, and CT scans. These services support quick diagnoses, aiding in effective treatment planning and patient care.

Patient Care and Facilities

We offer comprehensive patient care with services like 24/7 emergency care, ICU with ventilators, private and general wards, and advanced medical facilities. Our team ensures compassionate, round-the-clock attention for a comfortable recovery.

Insurance and Financial Services

We collaborate with multiple insurance providers to ensure smooth coverage for our patients, including private and government health insurance, and TPA services like Medicate, Mediassist, Raksha TPA, Star Health, Max Bupa, and TATA AIG, offering hassle-free claims.
