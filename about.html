Know us

Committed to quality healthcare and patient satisfaction.

Established in 2005 by Dr. T V Tyagaraj, MS in Orthopaedic Surgery, Aditya Orthopaedic & Trauma Center is a leading hospital located in Tumkur. Renowned for advanced orthopaedic and trauma care, we prioritize excellence in treatment and patient well-being.

  • Specialized care for premature and critically ill newborns.
  • Fully equipped with advanced technology for safe and efficient surgeries.
  • Providing high-quality, customized prosthetic limbs to restore mobility and improve quality of life for amputees.
  • Tailored rehabilitation programs to restore movement and strength post-surgery or injury.

Frequently Ask Question

  • What medical services are available at your hospital?

    We offer a wide range of services including Casualty & Emergency, MICU, NICU, General Surgery, Orthopedics, Plastic Surgery, and more.

  • Are 24-hour medical services available?

    Yes, we provide 24/7 medical services to ensure immediate attention for emergencies at any time.

  • Do you offer insurance coverage?

    We accept both private and government health insurance, ensuring seamless claims processing for various providers like Star Health, Tata AIG, and more.

  • What are your orthopedic services?

    Our orthopedic services include joint replacement, trauma care, spine surgery, and treatment for musculoskeletal disorders, backed by expert surgeons.

  • Is physiotherapy available?

    Yes, we provide comprehensive physiotherapy services to support recovery, mobility, and pain relief for patients of all ages.

  • What types of surgeries are performed?

    We offer a wide range of surgeries, including general surgery, laparoscopic surgery, plastic surgery, and neurosurgery, all performed by experienced specialists.

  • What is the NICU service?

    Our Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) provides specialized care for critically ill newborns, ensuring their health and safety under expert medical supervision.

  • How can I avail ambulance services?

    Our ambulance services are available 24/7 for emergencies, equipped with necessary medical facilities to transport patients safely.
